What is GTM Decay?

GTM Decay is the gradual erosion of revenue metrics caused by go-to-market workflows built in isolation and left ungoverned.

What are the four pillars of GTM Infrastructure?

Context: ICP profiles, battle cards, and buying signal logic. Data: clean, connected, measurable contact records and CRM data. Process: playbooks, handoffs, rep interaction patterns, and review cadences. Technology: the specific tools each workflow runs on, configured correctly for that workflow.

What is a Revenue Operating System (rOS)?

A Revenue Operating System (rOS) is the governed state of GTM operations — the opposite of GTM Decay. Built and governed by RevShoppe, it establishes the four GTM Infrastructure pillars, runs AI-powered workflows built for specific ICP and use case combinations, and stays in an ongoing optimization rhythm so GTM Decay cannot return.

What makes a GTM Workflow different from a sequence?

A sequence stamps the same message and forgets. A GTM workflow is a triggered, end-to-end process that reads buying signals, qualifies fit, gathers live context, generates a relevant output via Claude, routes to a human for approval, executes, and logs the outcome. Every run starts sharper than the last.
