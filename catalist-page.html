What Catalist Tracks

Four categories of signals your competitors aren't monitoring.

Catalist doesn't monitor digital behavior. It tracks the real-world events that change a company's operating environment and by extension their buying timeline.

01 · Trigger
Trigger Catalists

Events that force immediate decisions. Facility activity, leadership changes, expansion permits, equipment installations. High-intensity signals with a direct buyer response and a short action window.

02 · Regulatory
Regulatory Catalists

OSHA and EPA updates, compliance deadlines, code changes, audit triggers. Regulatory pressure creates urgency that no intent signal manufactures. When a company has a 90-day compliance window, the buying decision is not optional.

03 · Market
Market Catalists

Cost spikes, competitive moves, incentive programs, market disruptions. External forces that shift buying timelines regardless of whether a buyer visited your pricing page.

04 · Operational
Operational Catalists

Internal conditions that signal capacity strain, workflow gaps, or system failure. Scheduling conflicts, production bottlenecks, integration breakdowns. The events your competitors aren't monitoring because they don't show up in an intent platform.
