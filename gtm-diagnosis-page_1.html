What We Diagnose

Every GTM problem lives in one of four pillars.

GTM Decay sets in when one or more of these four pillars degrades and nobody owns fixing it. A sequence last updated 18 months ago. 230,000 records with no lead source. Every BDM-to-AE handoff living in Slack DMs. That's how it happens — slowly, then compounding. The Diagnosis audits all four pillars, finds where the decay set in, and attaches a dollar amount to each one.

01 Context

Who your best buyer is, what signals say they're ready to engage, and what the AI or the rep needs to know to run a Workflow correctly. ICP profiles, messaging frameworks, battle cards — the structured information layer your commercial motion reads before it does anything.

What we typically find
9 of 10 sequences we audit are regional, not persona-specific. Reply rate: 0.7%. Industry median is 2–3%. The message is the bottleneck, not the volume.
ICP profiles · Battle cards · Signal logic · Messaging
02 Data

Clean, connected, and measurable. Contact records accurate enough to engage, CRM structured so attribution can be tracked, and outcome data tied to the activities that produced it. Without this, you can't score leads, report ROI, or know which workflows are working.

What we typically find
80%+ contacts missing job function, level, and title. 230,000 records with no lead source. 100% of opportunities missing sequence attribution. Any segmentation built on this data is fiction.
Contact quality · Lead source · CRM structure · Attribution
03 Process

Playbooks, rep interaction design, handoff protocols, and review cadences — the operating model that makes Workflows repeatable. When Process breaks, results depend entirely on individual rep judgment. Your best performers compensate. Everyone else doesn't know how.

What we typically find
~2 touches before abandonment per account. 1,500+ accounts per BDM with zero prioritization signal. 100% of BDM-to-AE handoffs in notes and Slack. When a rep leaves, pipeline intelligence leaves with them.
Playbooks · Rep enablement · Handoff design · Cadences
04 Technology

The specific tools each Workflow runs on — configured for that Workflow, not the generic setup your vendor shipped. Having Outreach isn't having a Workflow. Technology fails when it's deployed but not wired to the other three pillars.

What we typically find
11,707 overdue tasks across one org. 10,000 sync errors in three days. Intent platform configured by vendor, never touched since. 43–54% of mailboxes and calendars disabled or misconfigured.
SEP · CRM · AI deployment · Integration audit
