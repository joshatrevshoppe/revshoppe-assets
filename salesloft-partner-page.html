Why it breaks

Salesloft executes the cadence. It does not define who it's targeting or why.

Salesloft handles cadence execution, meeting booking, call logging, and rep task management. What it cannot do is define the ICP criteria that determine which accounts are worth a cadence, verify that the underlying data is clean enough to act on, or design the process that governs whether a rep actually uses the platform the way it was built. Those three things — Context, Data, Process — are infrastructure problems. They sit outside Salesloft. And when they are unbuilt, Salesloft runs the same broken motion at scale.

Common questions

What organizations ask before they engage.

We have Salesloft running but cadence performance has plateaued. Where do we start?
With a GTM Diagnosis. RevShoppe assesses your Salesloft environment against the four GTM Infrastructure pillars — Context, Data, Process, Technology — and identifies which pillar is breaking the motion. In most cases, the platform is configured correctly. The gaps are in the three pillars that feed it. The Diagnosis quantifies what those gaps cost and blueprints what to build first.
How does RevShoppe build GTM Workflows with Salesloft?
A signal fires — an account shows a trigger event (funding round, leadership change, tech stack change, intent spike). Context assembles: company news, CRM history, ICP fit score, competitive positioning. Claude reads that context and generates a cadence step. A rep reviews and approves. Salesloft sends it. The outcome (meeting booked, no response, disqualified) feeds back into the context layer so every subsequent run is more precise. Salesloft is the delivery layer. RevShoppe architects the workflow that drives it.
Do you manage migrations from Outreach to Salesloft?
Yes. RevShoppe manages full migrations including cadence translation, CRM data integrity during cutover, team onboarding, and day-one workflow continuity. We also run a GTM Diagnosis in parallel with the migration so the new Salesloft environment is built on the right infrastructure — not a direct copy of what was not working in Outreach.
How does ongoing governance work after the initial build?
Governance is delivered through the Revenue Operating System (rOS) — RevShoppe's permanent engagement model. It includes a Center of Excellence (internal team capability), a Rhythm of Revenue (regular operating cadence), and ongoing Workflow Optimization. Salesloft governance sits inside the Technology pillar of rOS: cadence audits, admin hygiene, new workflow deployments, and quarterly performance reviews.
